A bit about me

I'm a software engineer who enjoys taking an ambitious idea and shipping it as a real, usable product. My core stack is Java & Spring Boot on the backend and React on the front end, and over the past while I've focused on applied AI — building reliable applications on top of large language models like GPT-4o.

That has meant designing clean REST APIs, wrangling messy real-world inputs (PDFs, CSVs, raw logs), engineering prompts for trustworthy structured output, and thinking about how AI agents are evaluated, not just demoed. I care about code that's readable, architecture that's simple, and software that actually solves the problem in front of it.

I hold a B.Tech from the College of Engineering, Pune (COEP). When I'm not building, you'll usually find me trekking somewhere in the Sahyadris.
